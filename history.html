History

Founding

Panic! At The Disco formed in 2004 by childhood friends, Ryan Ross and Spencer Smith. They both attended Bishop Gorman High School and began playing music together in the 9th grade. They invited there friend Brent Wilson from the nearby Palo Verde High School. Wilson then invited his classmate Brendon Urie.

Early Career

Without even playing live befor, Panic! At The Disco was already signed a recording contract. The band achieved this by sending a link to Fall Out Boy bassist Pete Wentz, that showed some of there early demos. Wentz was in L.A at the time with the rest of the band, and drove out to meet the band in Las Vegas, after listening to the band practice, they were impressed,and wanted Panic! to sign with there record label.
